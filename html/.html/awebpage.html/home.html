FOOD MEXO

Donut is a sweet, fried pastry that's typically ring-shaped with a hole in the middle. Donuts are a popular snack or breakfast food that can be topped with sprinkles and glazes. They are usually soft and doughy in texture.

Macarons are a type of French almond meringue cookie that is often filled with buttercream, ganache, or jam. They are known for their delicate texture and vibrant colors..

Ladoo Round balls of sweet dough, often made with semolina, flour, or lentils. They can be coated with nuts or coconut..

Gummy candies are a popular snack for children and adults alike. They are often enjoyed as a treat or as part of a larger candy assortment.
